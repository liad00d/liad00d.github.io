The Neighbourhood

About

The Neighbourhood is a Californian band that produces alternative rock music. Their members include Jesse Rutherford, Zachary Abels, Jeremy Freedman, Mikey Margot and Brandon Alexander Fried. They are known for their hit "Sweather Weather", giving them a rise to fame in 2013. The band is currently on a global tour, and will be performing all across North America in late 2019.

Hard To Imagine The Neighbourhood Ever Changing

Being the band's longest collection of songs, the album contains the original self titled album, "The Neighbourhood", as well as the multiple EPs they produced all in 2018, named "Hard", "To Imagine", and "Ever Changing". The combination of these titles makes up the final album name.
